Call of Duty: Black Ops 3

Classes and Specialists

Ruin

Ruin's special weapon is the Gravity Spikes. They are two small spikes that detonate upon impact on an enemy or contact with an enemy. His special ability is overdrive which boosts his leg speed. This is called overdrive.

Outrider

Outrider's special weapon is the Sparrow. Its a compound bow that fires bolts with increasing range the longer it is drawn. The bolt is explosive on contact. Her special ability is Vision Pulse. Its a sonic pulse that pings the surrounding area and reveals enemy within range.

Prophet

Prophet's special weapon is the Tempest. It fires electricity blasts at his enemies. His special ability is Glitch which allows him to flash back to a previous position however keeping previous efects.

Battery

Battery's special weapon is the 'War Machine. This is a grenade launcher that fires bouncing grenades which detonate after a short time. Her special ability is called Kinetic Armor. This is reactive armor that deflects enemy bullets away from the body for five seconds.

Sereph

Sereph's special weapon is the Annihilator. This is a one-shot kill pistol with great penetration power. Her special ability is called Combat Focus. While active, all score earned will trigger a bonus multiplier towards Scorestreaks.

Nomad

Nomad's special weapon is the H.I.V.E. This fires out pod traps that release nano drones upon enemies. The pods can be stuck on various surfaces in-game. His special ability is Rejack. This allows you to recover from the position of your death.

Reaper

Reaper's special weapon is the Scythe. This turns his arm into a quad barrel mini-gun. His special ability is called Psychosis. This allows him to create three decoys of himself that run forward.

Spectre

Spectre's special weapon is the Ripper. These are twin blades that give extended melee lunge. The rippers replace the standard melee attack, and can be used while holding your weapon. His special ability is Active Camo. As you would expect, this allows Spectre to become nearly invisible on the battlefield for a short time.

Firebreak

Firebreak's special weapon is the Purifier. This is a flamethrower that can scorch enemies within close range. His special ability is called Heat Wave. Firebreak will emit a massive heat blast that stuns enemies and destroys hostile equipment within range.
